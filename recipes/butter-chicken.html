Butter Chicken - Indian Style

Description - What is Butter Chicken?

Butter Chicken is love. It's an aromatic, creamy and flavorful indian cuisine that will resuscitate your taste buds. It can be enjoyed with simple steamed rice or a naan bread.

Salivating already? let's get started..

Ingredients - What do I need?

  • Oil and butter - Any oil, Any Butter works!
  • 1 medium onion - Diced in small chunks
  • tomato paste - chop and grind 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger - finely chopped (use paste if you need to)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 1 pound boneless chicken breast. Skinless and diced in 3/4 inch chunks
  • t tsp cumin, fenugreek, salt, black pepper
  • heavy cream - 1 cup
  • steamed rice or naam - chose your poison

Steps - How to actually cook it?

It's as easy as eating cereals with milk.. no I'm kidding, the only other thing that's as simple as cereals and milk is cereals and milk. But trust me, it's easier than you think. Don't waste your hard earned money on that Indian Restaurant.

  1. Heat a large saucepan on medium flame (c'mon use whatever you got, don't be a baby). Add some oil and butter (yes, both. I never said this going to be a diet friendly recipe). Heat it for 10 seconds and add cumin seeds, let it crackle and then add those finely chopped onions. Cook onions until golden brown (I said golden brown not charcoal black.. be careful masterchef!). Add some goodness of finely chopped garlic and ginger (or use the paste). That's what will give that blissful aroma. Let it all cook slightly for 30 seconds. Don't burn it.. please.

  2. Let's add in that tomato paste now. Let it blend with onions, garlic and ginger. Cook for 4-5 minutes and add your chicken chunks and all remaining spices (don't forget the salt as per your taste). Cook until everything is thoroughly cooked, should take about 10-12 minutes.

    3. Pro Tip: Use less salt, we can always fix it later..

  3. Add heavy cream and let it simmer for 10 more minutes. Keep stirring that amazing butter chicken. Voila! Your butter chicken is ready.

    4. Serve it hot over some steamed rice or naan bread.

    Enjoy!
